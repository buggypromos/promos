Services

Furniture and Wood Refinishing

Florida's Furniture Fixers specializes in wood and furniture refinishing. We can transform damaged finishes on tables, chairs, doors, and much more. We pick up and return, and some stationary items can be done on site.

Wood Touch Up

We are able to touch up wood damages to furniture and wood fixtures. Gouges, we are able to do a burn in permanent repair to fill the area, and tone and finish to complete. Most touch up can be done on site, depending on the damages.

Recliner and Sofa Repairs / Most Models

We are able to repair recliner and sofa frames, if parts need to be replaced such as a recliner mechanism, it would depend on the brand of the chair or sofa, if parts would be obtainable. We are able to order parts from Franklin, Bradington Young, Lane, Corinthian, and Leggett and Platt.
All wood frame repairs can be done on a sofa, recliner, loveseat, or ottoman, by repairing or replacing the wood. A knock down is a service that we provide for people that cannot fit a piece of furniture into a room because it is to large to fit in a doorway etc. We are able to undo joints to the frame to get into the room and then we can reassemble once furniture is in the desired room.

Commercial on Site Wood Restoration and Repair

Florida's Furniture Fixers also specializes in on site commercial, wood restoration and repairs. We go to court houses, doctors offices, any office that needs, chair arms recolored where the finish is missing, door scratches, desk damages to finish, trim work, any wood fixture in a office that needs on site touch up and repair, can be done by us.

Antique Restoration

We transform your family heirlooms to their original beauty. Tables, sewing machines, bedroom sets, and dining sets. We can totally restore or a clean up finishes depending on what the client desires.
